Problem Statement

With the onset of the COVID-19 pandemic, the Chadsey Condon Community Organization (CCCO) lost the ability to interact with the residents in their community through their walk-in office - a vital part of their organization that provided a one-stop destination for help and resources to community residents. After having to move their organization’s services and resources fully online, CCCO needed a full revamping of their website to cater to the new, powerful site utility necessary for their organization to successfully move forward and grow.

Solution

Our team designed a new website for CCCO which allowed them to smoothly transition their office to a virtual environment by facilitating increased and optimal communication with their residents, reorganizing their resources for easier search and navigation, and incorporating new features that increase their backend efficiency and their website’s utility.

Due to the limited resources available at Chadsey Condon and the organization’s ingrained usage of Squarespace, our team decided to continue using Squarespace, a web content management system (CMS) but created a new website that was more user-friendly and provided an interactive digital space for users.

Agile

Our team adopted an Agile development approach with evolving requirements and solutions split into sprints. We had four 2-3 week sprint cycles, with each sprint consisting of a planning and review phase with Chadsey Condon. The goal for each sprint was to identify issues and challenges to resolve and deliver a working product.

Key Features

Chat

The Facebook-powered Live Chat feature allows Chadsey Staff to quickly respond to resident questions and comments through their Facebook page.

Zone Map

Built using custom-created geographical maps, residents have access to information about important Landmarks within the Chadsey Condon neighborhood.

Resource Filter

Resource filtering buttons are set so user can easily find resources they need and which category they are viewing with the custom developed active state.

Booking Office Hours

A user can book a 30 minutes virtual office hour with Chadsey Condon through Calendly software platform.

Translation

The new site translations utilize the power of Google Translate to deliver website translations that are directed towards the target demographic of Chadsey Condon residents.

Report Activities

The reporting feature allows residents to notify Chadsey Condon of a multitude of issues ranging from potholes to illegal dumping around the neighborhood.

FAQ

The FAQs page provides answers to common questions that users often have, giving users an additional avenue to obtain answers they seek.

Newsletter

A service integration with Mailchimp to help Chadsey Condon manage their newsletters and keep their end users updated with latest community events.
