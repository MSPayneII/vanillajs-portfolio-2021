For this project, my team worked with Journeys International to help the company gain an understanding of how individuals younger than their current target audience approach adventure travel. We define adventure travel as a trip that promotes self-growth and unique experiential activities.

What is Journeys International?

Journeys International is a travel agency known for its highly customized travel itineraries. The company was founded by Joan and Will Weber in 1978 in an effort to provide more options for eco-friendly and culturally respectful travel. To this day, the company adheres to this mission. A local guide accompanies tourists on each trip, and Journeys collaborates with various non-profits in an effort to offset carbon output from air travel. Despite its small size of fewer than 20 employees, the Ann Arbor-based company has received several accolades from noteworthy publishers, including Conde Nast, Travel and Leisure, and National Geographic.

Objective

As the company pivots to expand its market, the goal was to understand the preferences and motivations of travel-inclined people aged 30-45 and provide recommendations for attracting this audience.
