This project started during my summer internship as a Citizen Interaction Design Summer Fellow in May 2020. As a fellow, I worked in interdisciplinary teams to provide solutions to Michigan municipalities and organizations' information issues. I served as an information professional but, more specifically, a user experience researcher. The project is ongoing, with a completion date near July 2021.

Michiganâ€™s Open Meetings Act (OMA) aims to promote transparency in government. Our group worked with the Michigan Township Association to provide recommendations on how Townships can conduct business meetings that are open to the public during the pandemic.

The Objective

Develop guidelines for the Michigan Township Association membership on how to host OMA-compliant meetings, notify the public of these meetings, and safely engage constituents during the pandemic.
